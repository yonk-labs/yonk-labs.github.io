{{/* Custom OpenGraph + Twitter Card emitter. LinkedIn only renders the large-hero preview card when og:image is close to 1.91:1 with explicit dimensions — the source feature images here are 1344x768 / 1376x768 (~1.75:1), which triggered the small thumbnail layout.
